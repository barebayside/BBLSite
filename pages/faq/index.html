Questions

Getting started

What's included in the free competitor snapshot?
You name one competitor. We score them across 8 channels — ads, reviews, SEO, social, pricing, website, service gaps, messaging — and email you a plain-English snapshot within 48 hours. No call, no card, no obligation. Same scoring rubric as our paid $517 Competitor Intelligence Report, just on one rival instead of five. If you like what you see, you can upgrade. If not, you keep the snapshot.
How long does setup take?
Intelligence reports are delivered within 48 hours of ordering. Full Lab Setup is delivered as a recipe library you build at your own pace — most customers complete it across 2–4 weekends.
Do I need to know Zoho?
No. We handle all the technical setup. After launch, we train you on the parts you need to use day-to-day (usually just checking a dashboard and responding to hot leads). Each recipe is click-by-click screenshots and plain-English steps — no code, no developer required.

Products & pricing

How much does it cost?
Intelligence reports start at $57 (Local Opportunity Report) and go up to $517 (Competitor Intelligence Report). Monthly monitoring subs start at $25/mo (Local Opportunity Monitoring). Bundles: Signal $45/mo, Intel $187/mo. Full Lab Setup — the complete marketing-infrastructure recipe library — is $1,597 flat. Right now the Competitor Intelligence Report and the free snapshot are live — the other reports, recipes and subscriptions are launching soon. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices. Check the services page for the full breakdown.
What makes you different from other agencies?
We build systems, not campaigns — and we're productized, not an agency. Every product is fixed-price with a documented deliverable. No retainers, no hourly billing, no strategy calls that go nowhere. Marketing infrastructure is delivered as self-build recipes; intelligence is delivered as reports. Most agencies run your ads for a month and leave. We build the infrastructure — CRM, automation, funnels, scoring — that keeps working whether we're involved or not.

Technical & account

What if I already have a CRM?
If you're on Zoho already, our recipes build on top of what you have. If you're on something else (HubSpot, Salesforce, spreadsheets), the Full Lab Setup covers migration as part of the setup. Tell us what you've got and we'll recommend the best path.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly services have no lock-in. If you're on a managed plan and you cancel, you keep everything we built — the CRM, automations, funnels, all of it. It's yours. Monthly monitoring subscriptions can be cancelled in one click, and you keep all data generated during your membership.
