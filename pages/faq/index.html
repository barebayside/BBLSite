HOW LONG DOES SETUP TAKE?

+
Intelligence reports are delivered within 48 hours of ordering. Full Lab Setup is delivered as a recipe library you build at your own pace — most customers complete it across 2-4 weekends.

DO I NEED TO KNOW ZOHO?

+
No. We handle all the technical setup. After launch, we train you on the parts you need to use day-to-day (usually just checking a dashboard and responding to hot leads). If you want us to manage it ongoing, that's an option too.

WHAT IF I ALREADY HAVE A CRM?

+
If you're on Zoho already, our recipes build on top of what you have. If you're on something else (HubSpot, Salesforce, spreadsheets), the Full Lab Setup covers migration as part of the setup. Tell us what you've got and we'll recommend the best path.

CAN I CANCEL ANYTIME?

+
Yes. Monthly services have no lock-in. If you're on a managed plan and you cancel, you keep everything we built — the CRM, automations, funnels, all of it. It's yours. You just won't have us managing and optimising it anymore.
