Questions people ask before they buy

What if my niche doesn't have many ads?

We pull what's live in your category, plus adjacent categories you could borrow angles from. If your niche genuinely has nothing worth collecting, we'll flag that before we start — so you never pay for a thin file.

Is this written ads, or the source to write from?

It's the source. We hand you the winning ads, hooks and posts already running — sorted, with a note on why each works — so you adapt them into your own creative. It's the swipe file you open before you write, not done-for-you ads.

No. Everything we collect is public — the live ads running in your category and the posts already out there. You just tell us your category and region.
