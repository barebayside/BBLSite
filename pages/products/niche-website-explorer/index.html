Questions people ask before they buy

Is this for my niche?

If other businesses in your category have websites we can pull and compare, yes. Trades, health, beauty, retail, professional services, hospitality, daycare, fitness all work. The one requirement is that your niche has top-performing sites worth taking apart.

Do I need a designer to use it?

No. The teardown is written in plain English, so you can act on it yourself. If you do use a builder, the prioritised fix list hands over with zero effort — it's a brief, not a hunch.

Do I have to give you access to anything?

No. Everything we analyse is public — the websites themselves and what's openly visible on them. You just tell us your niche and your own site.
