Is this for my industry?

If your business serves a local area (suburbs, not whole country), yes. Trades, health, retail, professional services, hospitality — all work. We've run it for 20+ categories.

If we genuinely find none worth acting on (rare — happens maybe 1 in 50 reports), you get a full refund AND keep the raw data. That's never the outcome though.

Suburb-level. We use exact postcodes and named suburbs. No guessing at state or city level.
