Questions people ask before they buy

Is this for my industry?

If your business serves a local catchment — suburbs, not the whole country — yes. Trades, health, beauty, retail, professional services, hospitality, daycare, fitness all work. The one requirement is that your customers search and review online, because that's the public demand we read.

What if there are no openings in my area?

There almost always are — we've never finished a report without flagging at least three. If your niche or suburb genuinely has nothing worth digging into, we'll flag that before we start so you don't waste the $47.

Do I have to give you access to anything?

No. Everything we analyse is public — searches, reviews, what rivals advertise and offer. You just tell us your category and suburbs.
