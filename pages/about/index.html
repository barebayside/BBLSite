Who this is for

Four people we built BBL for.

If you see yourself in any of these four, you're in the right place. If you don't — we'll tell you straight.

Segment 01

Small Business Owners

You're running a team of 1–10 people. You know you need better systems but you don't have the time to research tools, learn software, or build it yourself. You just need someone to make it work.

Sounds like you?

Leads fall through the cracks. Follow-ups are inconsistent. You're tracking everything in your head or a spreadsheet.

Segment 02

Local Service Businesses

Trades, clinics, studios, salons, restaurants — you're great at what you do, but the phone should be ringing more than it is. You're spending on ads and can't tell which ones work.

Sounds like you?

Your calendar has gaps. Happy customers don't leave reviews. You're paying for ads with no idea what they produce.

Segment 03 — New

Creators & Social Personalities

You're building an audience and watching rivals get ahead with ads or content you can't quite see. You want a clean read on what they're spending on, what they're testing, and what's actually landing — so you can leapfrog instead of guess.

Sounds like you?

You keep seeing a competitor's ads and wondering "why is this one working?" You want answers, not gut feel.

Segment 04 — New

Short-Window & Event Campaigns

Festivals, pop-ups, seasonal launches, election-style pushes — a wave of competitors all activating in the same 2–6 week window. You need a fast, current read on who's running what, where, and how loudly, so you don't get drowned out before the window closes.

Sounds like you?

Your campaign has a hard end-date. The noise is loudest right when it matters most. You need intel this week, not next quarter.
