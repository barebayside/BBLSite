## Publishing Order

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-12):** The first 8 articles already written — response and staple posts across the 3 original pillars.

**Phase 2 (Weeks 13-16):** First 3 pillar pages (Marketing Automation, Local Business, SEO) — they need spokes to link to.

**Phase 3 (Weeks 17+):** Pick from the bank above based on what's driving leads. Check Zoho CRM UTM data monthly — if the Zoho CRM articles bring in leads, write more Zoho CRM articles. If the sales psychology ones get shared on LinkedIn, write more of those. Let data guide the priority, not a calendar.

**The 1-article-per-week pace** works for the first 12 weeks. After that, consider 2 per week if you're seeing traction — one response post (quick, 1,000 words) and one staple post (deeper, 2,000 words) alternating.

At 1 article per week, this bank covers **4 years** of content. At 2 per week, **2 years**.
